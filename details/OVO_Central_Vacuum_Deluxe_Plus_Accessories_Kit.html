OVO Central Vacuum Deluxe Plus Accessories Kit

Elevate your cleaning routine with this premium collection of accessories designed for efficiency and performance. Whether you're maintaining hardwood floors or cleaning delicate furniture, our kit delivers the tools you need to make the job easier and more effective.

50ft Low-Voltage Hose with On/Off Switch

Our 50ft low-voltage hose is engineered for smooth operation and includes an on/off switch for precise control. This feature ensures your hose remains flexible, preventing kinks and allowing uninterrupted cleaning throughout your home.

12-Inch Premium Pivoting Neck Floor Brush

Expertly crafted with soft horse hair bristles, the 12-inch pivoting neck floor brush is perfect for gathering dust and debris without damaging your hard floors. Its flexible neck allows for easy maneuvering in tight corners and around furniture.

Telescopic Wand with Button Lock

The telescopic wand offers adjustable reach with a secure button lock mechanism, making it ideal for cleaning high surfaces, upholstery, and hard-to-reach areas with ease and precision.

Soft Bristles Brush Assortment

Effortless Storage and Organization

Complete your cleaning setup with the deluxe accessory bag and chrome hose hanger, which keep your tools organized and clean. This eliminates the hassle of searching for misplaced items and keeps your work area clutter-free.

Ideal for Everyday Cleaning

This central vacuum kit is built for everyday use, providing efficient maintenance and easy access to essential tools. Perfect for homeowners who want a reliable, convenient cleaning solution that adapts to their lifestyle.
